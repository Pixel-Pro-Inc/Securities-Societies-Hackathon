Process Outline

The society registration process is divided into two stages:

  1. Application for Name Authorisation
  2. Application for Registration

1. Stage One

Application for Name Authorisation

At this stage you will apply for a name for your society. We will check if your desired name is available and valid and reserve it for you until you either finish stage 2 or 90 days ellapse.

The list of requirements for this stage are provided below when you are ready to proceed click

Requirements for all organizations

  • Letter of intention to register a society (PDF format)
  • Minutes of meeting of formation of the society (PDF Format)
  • Attendance Register (with full names, identity numbers, contact numbers and signatures) (PDF format)
  • List of 10 proposed Names for the society
  • List of Three or more Objectives
  • Authorization from parent organization (where applicable) (PDF Format)
  • Certified copy of constitution of parent organization (where applicable) (PDF format)

Additional documents for Religious Organizations

  • Theology certificate from an accredited institute (PDF format)
  • Ordination certificate for the church Spiritual Leader (PDF Format)

2. Stage Two

Application for Registration

At this stage you complete the registration process. We will assess your entire application and send you your certificate of registration.

The list of requirements for this stage are provided below. Once stage 1 reaches completion you will be able to start stage 2

Requirements for all organizations

  • Proposed Constitution, submitted in triplicate (PDF format)
  • Form A, submitted in triplicate (PDF Format)
  • Membership List (PDF format)
  • Application fee of P500.00
